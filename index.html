The Emperor's New Groove

The Emperor's New Groove is a 2000 American animated buddy slapstick comedy film created by Walt Disney Feature Animation. The film follows a teenage emperor who is transformed into a llama by his ex-advisor and henchman. In order for the emperor to change back into a human, he trusts a village leader who escorts him back to the palace.
